📋 SPECTRUM® 22 SDS

Identification

Product:SPECTRUM® 22 — Antimicrobial for poultry (7 CFR 205.605)
Emergency:+1 800 424 9300 (CHEMTREC 24hr)

Composition

Peracetic acid:20–23% (CAS 79-21-0)
Hydrogen peroxide:8.5–10.5% (CAS 7722-84-1)
Acetic acid:33–39% (CAS 64-19-7)

Hazards — DANGER

  • Heating may cause fire (Organic Peroxide Type F)
  • Corrosive to metals; severe skin burns and eye damage
  • Harmful if swallowed, skin contact, or inhaled

First Aid

Eyes:Rinse several minutes. Get medical attention immediately.
Skin:Remove clothing. Wash 15+ min. Get medical attention.
Inhalation:Fresh air. Oxygen if labored breathing.
Ingestion:Do NOT induce vomiting. Large water. Get medical attention.

Transport

UN Number:UN 3109 — Class 5.2 | Sub: 8

📋 PERSYNT 500 Super D SDS

Identification

Product:PERSYNT 500 Super D — 50% Hydrogen Peroxide (Evonik/Univar)

Hazards — DANGER

  • May intensify fire — Oxidizer (Class 5.1)
  • Harmful if swallowed or inhaled
  • Severe skin burns and eye damage; corrosive to metals

First Aid

Eyes:Rinse 15+ min. Get medical attention immediately.
Skin:Rinse 15+ min. Get medical attention immediately.
Inhalation:Fresh air. Get medical attention if symptoms persist.
Ingestion:Rinse mouth. Do NOT induce vomiting. Call doctor immediately.

Storage

Below 104°F. Away from combustibles, metals, reducing agents. Ensure proper venting.

Transport

UN Number:UN 2014 — Class 5.1 | Sub: 8
Conversion:Weight ÷ 10 = Gallons
🚨 UN 3109 — ERG Guide 145
🚨 ERG GUIDE 145 — ORGANIC PEROXIDES (Heat & Contamination Sensitive)

Fire / Explosion

  • May explode from heat, shock, friction, or contamination
  • May ignite combustibles; containers may explode when heated

Isolation

  • Spill: 50 meters (150 ft) all directions
  • Fire: 800 meters (½ mile) all directions

Fire Response

  • NO dry chemicals, CO₂, or Halon — flooding water from distance
  • Withdraw if rising sound or discoloration — may EXPLODE

Spill Response

  • Full PPE required. Prevent runoff to sewers/waterways.

First Aid

  • Fresh air; call 911. Flush skin/eyes 20+ min. Keep victim calm and warm.
🚨 UN 2014 — ERG Guide 140
🚨 ERG GUIDE 140 — OXIDIZERS | UN 2014: Hydrogen Peroxide

Fire / Explosion

  • Accelerates burning; may explode from heat or contamination
  • May ignite combustibles; containers may explode when heated

Isolation

  • Spill: 50 meters (150 ft) all directions
  • Fire: 800 meters (½ mile) all directions

Fire Response

  • NO dry chemicals, CO₂, or Halon — flooding amounts of water
  • Withdraw if rising sound or discoloration — TANK MAY EXPLODE

Spill Response

  • Flush with large amounts of water. NO combustible absorbents.
  • Prevent entry into waterways, sewers, confined areas

First Aid

  • Fresh air; call 911. Flush skin/eyes 20+ min. Keep victim calm and warm.
